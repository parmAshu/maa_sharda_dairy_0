Our Cows

At the heart of our dairy lies the Sahiwal, a legendary breed originating from the fertile plains of the Punjab. The Sahiwal is globally recognized as the finest milch breed of the Indian subcontinent.

We treat our herd with the respect they deserve, blending traditional Vedic care with modern hygiene standards to bring you milk exactly as nature intended.

  • Sahiwal cows naturally produce milk containing only the A2 beta-casein protein. This makes our milk easier on the stomach, reducing bloating and ensuring better nutrient absorption for your entire family.
  • Sahiwal milk is famous for its high fat content (4.5% - 5.5%) and rich mineral profile. It is the secret behind our aromatic, golden-yellow Ghee and thick, creamy dahi.

Why Choose A2 Milk?

A2 Protein - Better Digestion

Sahiwal Cow Milk has good amount of A2 protein which is much needed for body maintenance and growth. It is easier on the stomach compared to regular A1 milk, reducing bloating and discomfort.

Improves health

Sahiwal Cow's Milk is rich in nutrients like omega-3 fatty acids, antioxidants etc.

Stronger Bones

Sahiwal Cow's A2 Milk is rich in calcium that is essential for strong bones.

No Hormones or Preservatives

We do not inject hormones to our cows for milk. Neither do we add preservatives to our products

Organic and Natural

We feed natural balanced diet to our cows. We also feed our cows natural herbs and avoid drug use. Our cows roam about freely. This improves cow's healt and milk's quality.

Creamy texture and golden hue

Desi cow milk can be identified by its golden hue. The golden hue is due to beta-carotene which is a natural antioxidant.

Hygienic and clean

Our milking process is extremely hygienic and well monitored. Our milkers clean and sanitize themselves before milking. Udders are cleaned thoroughly before and after milking. Antiseptic solutions are used to clean udders after milking and calf is done feeding. All containers are thoroughly cleaned with sanitzed with hot water or steam.

Ethical

At our farm our cows are treated like are mothers. Our cows are fed natural diet, natural herbs and have access to clean drinking water at all times. Our cows roam freely and we maintain clean living conditions for them. Ours calves are well cared for and fed sufficient milk till needed. We donot abandon our Gaumata at any time. Once they enter our farm they live with us till the end.

Suddh (Pure)

Only desi cow's milk and by-products can be used for dharmic use.
